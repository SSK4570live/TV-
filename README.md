🌟 About LIVE TV+

- LIVE TV+ is an open-source IPTV project created to make live television freely accessible to everyone.

- Our goal is to deliver a premium streaming experience with high-quality live TV channels, smooth playback, and minimal buffering while keeping the service completely free.

---

✨ Features

- 📺 Thousands of Live TV Channels
- 🎬 Premium Streaming Experience
- ⚡ Fast & Stable Performance
- 💨 Low Buffer Streaming*
- 🌍 Multiple Channel Categories
- 📱 Works with Most IPTV Players
- 🔄 Frequently Updated Playlists
- 🎯 Simple & User-Friendly

«* Streaming quality depends on your internet connection and the source availability.»
